IN THE SPOTLIGHT
the Worker 650 harbor service

Equipped with a 57hp inboard diesel engine, a ‘cutter suction dredger’, and a towing bollard, this Worker 650 is indispensable in North Beveland harbor (NL). Due to ebb and flow, the harbor tends to silt up. The cutter suction dredger features a rotating head that loosens the hard surface. The Worker 650 frequently rescues other boats in distress with the towing bollard.
